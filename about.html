Exactly who am I?

I’m Pavitra Kannan, a passionate and adaptable software developer with a strong inclination toward UI/UX design, cloud technologies, and real-world problem-solving through code. I love creating user-centric web applications that blend functionality with great design. From developing responsive websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to implementing machine learning models with Python and scikit-learn, I enjoy turning concepts into working prototypes.

I’ve earned certifications in AWS Cloud Architecting, UI/UX Foundations (Google), and completed multiple internships in Web Development and Machine Learning.

I’m also proud to have published 3 research papers, and I’ve participated in national-level hackathons and competitions like Innovision and the NASA Hackathon.

I strive to be a well-rounded developer who’s not only tech-savvy but also collaborative, communicative, and constantly curious. Whether I’m coding, designing, or performing, I bring passion, precision, and a desire to grow.
